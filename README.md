# Web-Scraping-ENAHO-2004-2024

In Stata, this script provides an efficient and comprehensive way to download, organize, and process Peru’s main household survey (ENAHO) for all available years from 2004 to 2024.

The code automates the download of all survey modules directly from official sources. In addition, it extracts compressed (.zip) files, enabling a streamlined and reproducible data pipeline. However, in cases where the compressed files contain issues or inconsistencies, the extraction process must be performed manually.

### Modules

This script includes the treatment of the following modules:

1. **Module 1** – Housing and Household Characteristics
2. **Module 2** – Household Members Characteristics
3. **Module 3** – Education
4. **Module 4** – Health
5. **Module 5** – Employment and Income
6. **Module 7** – Food and Beverages Expenditure (Module 601)
7. **Module 8** – Charitable Institutions
8. **Module 9** – Housing Maintenance
9. **Module 10** – Transport and Communications
10. **Module 11** – Housing Services
11. **Module 12** – Recreation, Entertainment, and Cultural Services
12. **Module 13** – Clothing and Footwear
13. **Module 15** – Transfer Expenditures
14. **Module 16** – Furniture and Household Equipment
15. **Module 17** – Other Goods and Services
16. **Module 18** – Household Equipment
17. **Module 22** – Agricultural Production
18. **Module 23** – Agricultural By-products
19. **Module 24** – Forestry Production
20. **Module 25** – Expenditures on Agricultural and/or Forestry Activities
21. **Module 26** – Livestock Production
22. **Module 27** – Livestock By-products
23. **Module 28** – Expenditures on Livestock Activities
24. **Module 34** – Summary (Calculated Variables)
25. **Module 37** – Social Programs (Household Members)
26. **Module 77** – Income of Self-Employed Workers
27. **Module 78** – Personal Care Goods and Services
28. **Module 84** – Citizen Participation
29. **Module 85** – Governance, Democracy, and Transparency

Overall, this tool enables the construction of a harmonized longitudinal database, facilitating economic analysis, labor market studies, poverty measurement, and public policy evaluation in Peru.
